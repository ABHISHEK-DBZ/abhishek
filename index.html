About Me

My journey into 3D began not in an art studio, but in the structured world of Electronic Engineering. This technical foundation in VLSI, data analytics, and cloud computing provides me with a unique perspective on creative challenges.

I see animation as a powerful tool for problem-solving—whether it's visualizing complex data, simulating real-world systems, or architecting new digital worlds. My goal is to build experiences that are not only visually stunning but also intelligent, efficient, and purposeful.

The Engineer-Artist

My background allows me to bridge the gap between technical complexity and creative expression. I thrive on projects that demand both left-brain logic and right-brain artistry.
